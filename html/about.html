About Us



Our Mission

  • Increase access to high-quality education for everyone, everywhere who is interested in finding the most cutting edge and artistic approaches for carving pumpkins.
  • Enhance teaching and learning on campus and online
  • Advance teaching and learning through research and innovation

Our Story

Our company started out of a class of 4 junior web developers at the New York Code and Design Academy in 2016. The class was simply given the task of collaborating on a website for a project. With the Halloween season in sight we considered a pumpkin carving academy. This first started as a joke but further went on to develop into the business we now have today. As we continue to write our story, change lives and carve pumpkins, we are thrilled to continue developing more expert carvers!

Our Diverse Community

Our students come from all over the country! Whether you are a halloween extremist or a regular, who is just curious about carving, we have the right course for you.
